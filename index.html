About the Workshop

This workshop gathers researchers and advanced students working on Hodge theory, singularities, and related areas of algebraic geometry and topology. Short talks, two keynote lectures, and discussion sessions are scheduled to encourage collaboration and knowledge exchange.

Example mathematical announcement: We welcome contributed talks on topics such as mixed Hodge modules, perverse sheaves, deformation theory of singularities, and applications to mirror symmetry. Example inline math: $H^k(X,\\mathbb{Q})$ and vanishing cycle functors $\\phi_f$.
